Rules and Regulations

  • Participants must submit their papers electronically on or before the deadline, which is April 23rd.
  • Each participant may submit only one paper for consideration.
  • The event is open to students, researchers, professionals, and enthusiasts in the relevant field.
  • All submitted papers must be original work and must not have been previously published or presented elsewhere.
  • Papers should present original research, innovative ideas, case studies, or literature reviews related to the field of interest.
  • Submitted papers will undergo a peer review process conducted by a panel of experts or reviewers.
  • Reviewers will evaluate papers based on criteria such as originality, clarity, relevance, methodology, and significance of findings.
  • Presenters should arrive at the designated venue early to set up their presentation materials and equipment.
